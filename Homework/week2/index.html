Hawaiian Style Mochi Donuts

Mochi Donuts include rice flour which makes them uniquely chewy with a pillowy soft inside and a perfectly crisp outside. It's our job to bring JOY to your life, one tasty mochi donut at a time. We put a lot of thought, love, and care into creating the tastiest combinations and seasonal flavors. So come by and check out what these en-JOY-able mochi donuts are all about!

ABOUT US
Walk-ins, Pre-order, and Delivery

We're operating out of a licensed commercial kitchen inside the VFW in Noblesville. We have 16 flavors everyday: 8 standard flavors and 8 flavors that rotate daily. We make our mochi donuts fresh every morning. Our doors open for walk-ins at 7:30am on Thursday, Friday, and Sunday. Pre-order for same day pick up and delivery is available Thursdays and Fridays beginning at 9:00 am.
